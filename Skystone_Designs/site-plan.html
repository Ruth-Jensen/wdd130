Overview

Purpose

Not everyone has artistic talents, however a big part of business is how visually appealing a person’s, or company's product is. There are also people out there that want art unrelated to a job such as people who want the character designed, or a tattoo they like. I help people take what they are imagining in their head and put it on paper.

Audience

My target audience are people seeking personalized character designs. The main people using my site will be individuals with a penchant for creativity and storytelling, my target audience includes aspiring writers, gamers, and other creative individuals who struggle to create visual content for their ideas. I will also do commissions for people who what art unrelated to character design, but they will not be my focus.

Content

Home page

Hello! Welcome to Skystone Designs, where your creative journey begins!

My turnaround time for commissions is flexible and tailored to your communication pace. On average, projects are completed within a week or two, but this timeframe can adjust based on your responsiveness.

I primarily do requests for character designs and concept art, however I do also specialize in crafting logos, tattoos, and more. Simply head to the commissions page to kickstart your project. Please note, I do not accept commissions for explicit content.

Prices start at $30 for logos and simple designs, $40 for tattoo designs, $50 for background design and $60 for character designs. For commissions not listed, an estimate will be sent via email for you to approve before I begin your project. You are allowed to request up to two revisions of the project; after that, it will be $10 extra per revision. Let's bring your ideas to life!

Commissions and FAQ Page

To ensure your commissioned character comes to life just as you envision, please provide key details such as:

  • Gender
  • Specific Character Traits (hair color, eye color, skin tone)
  • A brief character description, including personality traits or the setting they belong to (medieval, modern, futuristic)
  • Be sure to detail clothing, accessories, and any other elements crucial to the character's appearance
  • for a more accurate representation, consider offering additional specifics. Whether your character is a tomboy or a graceful figure, a sports enthusiast or a bookish nerd

If there are aspects you're flexible about, I'll happily improvise. Your input helps shape their visual identity. Remember, the more details you provide, the closer I can get to realizing your unique vision. Feel free to leave out details if accuracy isn't a top priority, but for those seeking a specific look, providing extra information can make a significant difference in the final result. Let's collaborate to bring your ideas to artistic fruition!
