Hello Everyone! I'm Skystone

Your friendly artist with a passion for bringing creativity to life! When I'm not immersed in the world of art, you can find me exploring the depths of Dungeons & Dragons, conjuring characters and weaving tales that spark the imagination.

But let me tell you a little secret, I'm not just about D&D! I'm also your go-to person for a whole range of artistic endeavors. From crafting captivating character designs and eye-catching logos to inking out stunning tattoos and painting mesmerizing background art, I'm your one-stop shop for all things artistic!

And here's the cherry on top, I absolutely adore taking commissions for fellow D&D enthusiasts! There's something truly magical about bringing someone's character to life on the canvas, infusing every stroke with personality and charm.

So, whether you're looking to dive into the world of D&D or you're in need of some killer character designs or logos, I've got you covered! Let's collaborate and make some art that'll leave you speechless!

About Me

See some of my projects

I primarily do requests for character designs and concept art, however I do also specialize in crafting logos, tattoos, and more. Simply head to the commissions page to kickstart your project.

Please note, I do not accept commissions for explicit content.

My turnaround time for commissions is flexible and tailored to your communication pace. On average, projects are completed within a week or two, but timeframe can adjust based on your responsiveness.

You are allowed to request up to two revisions of the project; after that, it will be $10 extra per revision. Let's bring your ideas to life!

Prices start at

  • $30 for logos and simple designs
  • $40 for tattoo designs
  • $50 for background design
  • $60 for character designs

For commissions not listed, an estimate will be sent via email for you to approve before I begin your project.
