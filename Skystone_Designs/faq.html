FAQ

**Can I request revisions?**

Absolutely! First two times are on the house, and after that, it's $10 extra per tweak.

**Any content guidelines?**

Yep, keeping it classy – no swear words or nudity.

**What payment methods?**

Credit Card, Debit Card, Bank Transfer, or Venmo – your call.

**How much for a commission?**

Starting at $30 for logos, $40 for tattoos, $50 for background design and $60 for characters. Got something unique? Estimate via email before we dive in.

**How do we chat during the process?**

Hit me up via email, phone call, or text – keeping it easy for your creative journey!

**How long does it take for a project to be completed?**

Flexibility is key! On average, projects wrap up within a week or two, but it's all about your pace and responsiveness. Let's make the creative journey enjoyable for you!
